## How to Redeem Far Far West Codes Correctly

1. Open **Far Far West** and load into your usual menu/hub.
2. Locate the **promo/code input area** (this may move after updates).
3. Paste the code directly (avoid manual typing if possible).
4. Submit and wait for confirmation text.
5. Check your account rewards immediately (especially ticket count).

After redemption, apply tickets through your upgrade path:

1. Go to **Weapon Shop** (or your hero loadout panel).
2. Select the weapon you’re actively leveling.
3. Click **Customize**.
4. Use **Free Levels** with your ticket stack.
5. Confirm each level until your target breakpoint is reached.

| Redemption Error | Likely Cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| **“Invalid code”** | Typo, wrong capitalization, hidden spaces | Paste code from trusted source and remove spaces before/after. |

> **Tip:** Keep a small text note with “tested today” dates. This prevents repeated attempts on dead codes.
