## Weapon System Basics You Need Before Choosing

Before ranking anything, lock in the systems that decide value:

- **Every weapon takes 6 fragments to unlock**
- You can **track fragments** for one weapon at a time
- Late-game strength comes from:
  - Base stat upgrades (up to 20 slots)
  - Joker loadouts (slot-limited by rarity)
  - Unique joker synergy (usually the biggest power spike)

| System | Why It Matters | Practical Rule |
|---|---|---|
| **Fragments** | Controls unlock speed | **Track 1 weapon at a time** to avoid split progress |
| **Stat points** | Defines baseline DPS/handling | Prioritize **damage + fire rate** unless weapon has a special scaling stat |
| **Joker slots** | Limits build freedom | Save room for **1-2 unique jokers** on core weapons |
| **Element interactions** | Multiplies team damage | Pair one weapon for **status application**, one for **detonation/burst** |

## Fastest Weapon Unlock and Leveling Route

Weapon power means little if unlocks stall. Follow this farm sequence:

| Priority | Fragment Source | Reliability | Time Efficiency |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | **Boss kill** | **High** | Best consistent gain per run |
| **2** | **Forge event (hammer icon)** | **High** | Strong if route planned at mission start |
| **3** | **Loot coyote** | Medium | Great bonus when encountered |
| **4** | **Well event** | Low | Small chance; do it if nearby only |

### Step-by-step grind plan

1. **Track one weapon** until 6 fragments are complete  
2. Enter missions with a route that includes **boss + forge**
3. Chase loot coyote only if detour risk is low
4. Spend first levels on your intended role stats (not random spreads)
5. Unlock unique jokers quickly—this is where most weapons spike

> **Tip:** If your goal is the **Far Far West best gun** for ranked difficulty pushes, prioritize unlock speed over trying every weapon early.

A lot of players lose tempo by unlocking too many side options. In 2026, focused progression is still the fastest way into high-tier builds.

## Common Mistakes When Picking the Far Far West best gun

Even high-skill shooter players can misbuild in this game because of joker slot economy.

- **Mistake 1: Overvaluing base stats, undervaluing unique jokers**  
  A mediocre gun can jump tiers with the correct unique.

- **Mistake 2: Ignoring secondary synergy**  
  Your secondary should patch range, ammo, or status gaps.

- **Mistake 3: Running two competing play patterns**
