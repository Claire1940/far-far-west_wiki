## Mission-Specific Optimization for Better Weapon Glory

Different objectives reward different pacing. If your performance drops between modes, your rotation is probably static when it should adapt.

### 1) Escort dynamite cart

You stand on the cart to move it, and enemies pressure lanes around your path. Here, weapon glory is about **area denial and clean lane resets**.

- Use boomerang on approach lines before enemies stack
- Save sheriff stars for high-priority threats that can disrupt positioning
- Maintain forward-facing control and avoid deep chases

### 2) Drill pressure objective

Valve management creates stop-start combat rhythm. Far Far West weapon glory here comes from **short burst cycles**:
- Clear immediate pressure
- Stabilize valves
- Re-engage at close-to-mid range

### 3) Magnet gold side objective

Target focus can break quickly because attention splits between interaction and combat.
- Assign one player (or yourself) to “threat-first” control
- Keep secondary ammo for interruption enemies
- Use mobility to retake control points without overextending

| Objective | Best Damage Rhythm | Recommended Secondary | Key Failure Point |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Escort Cart** | Continuous medium pressure | **Boomerang** | Leaving lane to chase one target |
| **Drill Pressure** | Burst-clear-utility loop | **Sheriff Stars** | Delayed elite confirms |
| **Magnet Gold** | Priority target snapping | Mixed (team dependent) | Split attention without role assignment |

> **Warning:** If you’re losing runs on “easy” due to tougher enemy mixes in 2026, your build is likely over-specialized. Shift to consistency-first and add burst through timing, not fragility.

2. **Second pass:** Swap one Joker for survivability/utility  
3. **Third pass:** Change secondary to match space control needs  

This protects your Far Far West weapon glory curve while reducing avoidable downtime.

| Modifier Pressure Type | What It Disrupts | Fastest Adaptation |
|---|---|---|
| **Falling hazard zones** | Stationary firing windows | Fire in short bursts, strafe reset every 2–3 seconds |
| **Tornado displacement** | Position holding and aim stability | Anchor near objective edges, avoid center overcommit |
| **Stacked modifiers** | Resource rhythm | Prioritize threat order over damage greed |

A good rule: if modifiers force you to move more, choose damage tools that function while rotating (boomerang paths, recoverable stars, burst windows after movement skills).

## Common Mistakes That Kill Weapon Glory

Even skilled players lose output through avoidable habits. Fix these first:

- Throwing sheriff stars into unsafe recovery zones  
- Using movement skills only as panic escape tools  
- Picking Jokers without objective context  
- Over-chasing kills during escort/drill timers  
- Entering boss phase with no planned burst order

Quick correction loop:
- Review one failed run
- Identify one repeated damage-loss event
- Replace one habit in next run only
- Track improvement over three matches

That process compounds faster than constant full-build swaps.
