At mission start, confirm you’re routing through the Far West area and not drifting into side objectives first. Keep mobility tools ready and don’t spend too much ammo before shard interactions.

### Step 2) First shard near the church (southeast side)

Your first shard location is near the church, behind it in the southeastern section. Interact with the bell sequence there. If you miss the order or mess up timing, reset and repeat immediately.

From church, move northwest a few hundred meters to a suspicious rock formation. You’ll see two zip lines rising to a central point. Take one zip line up and access the second bell area.

## Bellshot Explained: Practical Value in Real Combat

Bellshot is not just a novelty effect. Used properly, it supports aggressive headshot play while giving incidental sustain.

| Bellshot Element | Practical Behavior | Build Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Headshot Trigger Chance** | Can spawn healing ghost bell | Rewards precision, especially with accurate weapons |
| **Ghost Bell Interaction** | Shoot bell to receive healing | Gives clutch recovery between pushes |
| **Healing Value** | Small but meaningful per proc | Adds up in multi-wave encounters |
| **Stacking Potential** | Can stack up to a cap | Better for players who already run tweak-heavy builds |

In live fights, Bellshot performs best when you:

1. Keep crosshair at head level during medium-range engagements.  
2. Rotate between cover so you can safely shoot spawned ghost bells.  
3. Avoid tunnel vision—healing bells help only if you create a short window to trigger them.  
4. Use stable recoil options so your headshot rate stays consistent.

If your aim is still developing, Bellshot may feel inconsistent at first. That’s normal. It scales with your mechanics.

## FAQ

**Q: How long does the far far west bell quest take in a clean run?**

A: Most players can finish it in a short segment of the mission if they route directly: church shard, zip line shard, north beach shard, then church return. Extra fights and detours are what usually inflate time.

**Q: Do I need a specific weapon to complete the far far west bell quest?**

**Q: Is Bellshot good only for advanced players?**

A: It benefits advanced aim more, but it still has value for improving players because it encourages better head-level crosshair placement and rewards cleaner engagements with small healing opportunities.

**Q: Can I skip the final church bell after collecting all three shards?**
