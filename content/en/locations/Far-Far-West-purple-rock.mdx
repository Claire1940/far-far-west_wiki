Secret missions in the game are designed to test your observational skills and combat precision. Most of these missions reward players with weapon jokers—specialized items that apply buffs or unique effects to your gear. While the Far Far West purple rock symbols often serve as markers for these challenges, it is important to note that each map features distinct requirements.

## Navigating the Map Secrets

To successfully complete these missions, you must pay attention to environmental cues. Some areas, like the Canyon, require you to gather specific medallions, while others, like the Woodlands, involve interacting with mushrooms in a precise clockwise sequence.

### Essential Mission Steps
1. **Far West:** Visit the church in the southeast, climb the tower, and complete the bell-shooting mini-games.
2. **Canyon:** Collect four medallions by digging near marked mounds and clearing debris with explosives.
3. **Desert:** Pair the six child cacti with their corresponding parents to earn the explosive mine joker.
4. **Woodlands:** Follow the map sequence on the central stone to touch mushrooms in the correct order.
5. **Area 41:** Decipher the monoliths and input the sequence (Frontier, Sand, Cliffs, Trees) to unlock the final joker.

## Advanced Tips for Completionists

If you are struggling with the Far Far North map, remember that the snowman assembly mission requires patience. When rolling the body parts back to the cave, avoid dense terrain like tree lines, as the objects can easily become stuck, forcing a restart of the mission. Always take the cleanest path possible to ensure your progress is not interrupted.

The Area 41 mission is the final test of your knowledge of the game's geography. By recognizing the symbols associated with the different biomes—the frontier, sand, cliffs, and trees—you can quickly input the correct code and gain access to the Anti-Gravity Joker, which is useful for vertical mobility in tricky platforming sections.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the Far Far West purple rock symbols?**

A: The purple rock symbols are primarily found in Area 41. They serve as monoliths that provide hints for the final secret mission, requiring you to recall the order of the other maps: Frontier, Sand, Cliffs, and Trees.

**Q: Do these secret missions reset every run?**

A: Yes, most of these missions can be repeated, allowing you to farm gold or re-acquire specific jokers if you are testing different character builds for your 2026 playthrough.

**Q: Which secret mission is the hardest to complete?**

A: The Desert mission is often considered the most tedious due to the number of cacti (12 total) that must be relocated. The Far Far North snowman mission is also tricky because the large body parts can get stuck on environmental geometry.

**Q: Are there any secret missions I should skip?**

A: Most players find the Anti-Gravity Joker underwhelming for combat. While it is fun to use, it does not provide the same tactical advantage as the Bellshot or Pickpick jokers.
