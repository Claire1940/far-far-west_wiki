### Movement tips for object pushing

When handling physics objects in snowy terrain, your angle matters more than speed. Use short controlled pushes and frequent line checks toward Jerry’s position.

- Keep the piece slightly ahead of your character centerline.
- Correct angle every 10–15 seconds instead of forcing one long straight run.
- Use track direction to re-align if visibility gets messy.
- On slopes, approach from the higher side to avoid lateral roll-offs.

> **💡 Tip:** If a snowman piece starts drifting, stop pushing for a second and reset from a cleaner angle. You’ll lose less time than trying to “muscle through” bad alignment.
