## Step-by-Step Route for All Far Far West Canyon Grave Locations

This section is your full run path. Follow it in sequence for speed and consistency.

### 1) Cave Room Grave
Head to the cave entrance area and walk into the small chamber. The grave is in the back. This is usually your easiest first pickup and confirms your tracking is working.

### 2) Cliffside Split-Path Grave
From there, target the grave that can be reached in multiple ways:
- Follow the minecart track route, **or**
- Use the zipline access from the opposite side, **or**
- High-jump up cliff sections if your movement allows it.

All methods lead to the same side where the grave is located. If you miss jumps often, tracks/zipline is safer than brute-force cliff hopping.

### 3) Roof and Scaffolding Grave
Move to the settlement-like structures where roofs connect to wooden scaffolding. Climb upward in stages. The grave is at the top platform.

This is one of the trickier **Far Far West canyon grave locations**, mainly because panic movement causes slips. Slow down and do deliberate jumps.

### 4) Southwest Zipline Drop Grave
Find the southwest zipline line. Ride from either end, and when you pass over the small land section beneath, hop off to collect the grave below.

Timing matters here:
- Jump too early: you miss the platform.
- Jump too late: you overshoot and must reset.

### 5) Ravine Grave by Towers and Pipe Bridge
Travel to the zone with giant towers and a pipe bridge. Drop or descend into the ravine floor area to collect the grave.

This one is often straightforward once you identify the correct ravine section.

### 6) Rope-Climb Cliff Grave
Go to the nearby cliff where a rope gives vertical access. Climb up and claim the grave on top.

### 7) Bridge-Cross Ravine Grave
From #6, look across the ravine—you can often spot the next grave area. Cross the bridge and pick it up. This pair (#6 and #7) is one of the fastest back-to-back segments in the run.

### 8) Eastern Edge Hidden Grave
Move toward the eastern side of the canyon. A gravestone sits partially obscured behind rocks near the edge. Get close to the lip and sweep your camera low; this is a common miss.

### 9) Mine Interior Hidden Room Grave
Locate the mine entrance and follow the tracks inward. You may notice glow cues, but the grave itself is inside a side room rather than fully exposed in the main tunnel. Enter the room to register it.

Among all **Far Far West canyon grave locations**, this is arguably the most “sneaky” placement.

### 10) Train Wreck Backside Grave
Finish at the train wreck area. Go behind the wreck and nearby rocks toward the ocean-facing side. The final grave is easy to miss if you focus only on the wreck itself.

If your count is still 9/10, revisit #8 or #9 first—they are the most commonly skipped.
