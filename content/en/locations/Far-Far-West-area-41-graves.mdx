## Step-by-Step Walkthrough (All 10 Graves in Efficient Order)

Follow this exact sequence to minimize backtracking.

### 1) North cliff hidden house grave
Start by moving to the northern side and scanning the murky water edge. Find the narrow cliffside path and continue until you reach wooden boards that form a short jump puzzle. Beyond this section is a hidden house. Enter and claim the first grave inside.

### 2) Small island grave
From the first location, move toward the nearby isolated island in the central zone. The grave is exposed and typically easy to spot from the shoreline.

### 3) Piano overlook grave
Climb to the marked cliff overlook with a piano and rocking chairs. Check directly behind the piano setup for the grave.

### 4) Tire acid pool grave
Head toward the small canyon-side acid pool filled with scattered tires. Instead of crossing away, follow the pool’s length all the way to the far end. The grave sits at the back.

### 5) Antenna under-bridge grave
Go to the antenna tower area and drop beneath the wooden bridge structure below it. Search the underside for the next grave.

### 6) Zipline house crate-side grave
Climb back up near the antenna cliff and take the zipline toward the house. Enter the house, go over/around stacked crates, and check behind them for the grave.

### 7) Giant tower summit grave
From the same zone, cross the bridge and jump to the adjacent structure. Reach the next zipline access point, then **climb upward** rather than taking a line that launches you away. At the top platform near outhouse-like structures, collect the grave.

### 8) West shipwreck hull grave
Rotate to the western side of Area 41. Find the wrecked ship and enter the hull interior. The grave is inside the broken section.

### 9) Submerged house base grave
Move to the pair of final nearby sites. First, check under the partially submerged house in acid water and grab the grave at its lower side/base.

### 10) Mast-top ship grave
Finish at the nearby ship by climbing to the mast. Use a movement skill if available to speed this segment. The final grave sits at the top.
