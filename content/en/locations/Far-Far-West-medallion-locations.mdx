## Medallion #1 and #2: Sheriff + Gold Prospector

### Sheriff Medallion (Easy Pickup)

At the locked door zone, you can pick up the first medallion from the sheriff. This is your baseline objective confirmation and the quickest medal in the run.

### Gold Prospector Medallion (Under Bridge Dig Puzzle)

Move toward the under-bridge section. You’ll find the prospector trail and a shovel access point nearby. Multiple dig spots can appear like bait; the real medallion spot is the correct southern dig patch in that sub-area.

**Execution checklist:**
- Drop to under-bridge level
- Locate the prospector clue/body trail
- Grab shovel access
- Ignore decoy-feel spots and move to the correct southern patch
- Dig and collect medallion

> **Warning:** Don’t spend too long on random dig attempts. The prospector segment is one of the easiest places to waste 5+ minutes if you over-search.

If you’re repeating runs for speed, memorize environmental anchors (bridge supports, cliff edge angle, and southern ground texture) so the prospector medal becomes a 30–60 second stop.

## Medallion #3: Bobby the Dynamiteer Mine Sequence

The third entry in **Far Far West medallion locations** is in the mine path near broken bridge terrain. You’ll notice a shaft blocked by gray breakable rocks. This section is straightforward once you treat it as a linear demolition lane.

### How to clear it cleanly

1. Approach mine entrance near the broken bridge landmark.
2. Pick up or prep dynamite interaction.
3. Blow open each gray rock barrier in sequence.
4. Push through the tunnel until the medallion pickup appears.
5. Loot Bobby’s medallion and exit immediately.

| Mine Segment | Visual Marker | Action | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Entrance | Gray rock wall | Prime dynamite | Mistimed throw wastes cycle |
| Mid tunnel | Second barrier cluster | Repeat blast | Getting turned around in smoke/debris |
| Final chamber | Opened path + pickup | Grab medallion | Overclearing enemies and losing pace |

For most players, this is the most “mechanical” part of the route. If you’re comfortable with interaction timing, this step is very reliable.

## Medallion #4: Johnny Hawkeye Safe Puzzle (10 Bullseyes)

This is usually the slowest segment in **Far Far West medallion locations**, mainly because one or two targets are easy to miss.

You’ll find a safe with a visible count (starting at 10). Each bullseye you hit lowers the counter by one. Hit all 10 and the safe opens with Hawkeye’s medallion.

### Bullseye search pattern (recommended)

Use a clockwise scan centered on the safe:
- Start near ground-level targets around safe
- Sweep vertical targets (wood platforms/rocks)
- Check zipline underside
- Rotate to opposite side near mine cart endpoint
- Confirm high middle target above cart lane

| Target Zone | Likely Height | Scan Priority | Miss Chance |

| Behind safe on rock | Mid | Early | Medium |
| Under zipline | Mid | Early | Medium |

| Above mine cart middle lane | High | Final check | **Very High** |

> **Tip:** If the safe is stuck at “1,” immediately check elevated centerline angles above the carts before re-clearing nearby targets.

Once the count hits zero, open the safe and collect the final medal.
