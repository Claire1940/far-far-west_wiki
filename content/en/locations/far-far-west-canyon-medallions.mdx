## All 4 Medallion Locations (Step-by-Step)

### 1) Sheriff Medallion (Starting Anchor)

Near the southern-middle section of the canyon map, you’ll find the locked door and sheriff clue area. The first medallion is tied to the sheriff, making it your easiest pickup. Treat this as your anchor point since you’ll return here at the end anyway.

**Execution notes:**

- Use this moment to orient your map for the next two vertical routes.

### 2) Gold Prospector Medallion (Under-Bridge Dig Objective)

This section sits below a bridge/cliff line. You’ll find the prospector context and digging points, but not every dig marker is correct. The correct medallion dig point is not the obvious trap pattern many players test first.

**Execution notes:**
- Drop down under the bridge route.
- Acquire/confirm shovel interaction point in the same objective chain.
- Prioritize the correct southern dig location in that zone.

### 3) Dynamiteer Medallion (Mine Breach)

The third medallion is in a mine path blocked by gray rock segments. You’ll need to use dynamite to open successive barriers.

**Execution notes:**
- Locate mine entrance under broken-bridge vicinity.
- Grab and light dynamite safely.
- Clear rock layers in sequence.
- Push through the tunnel until medallion pickup appears.

### 4) Hawkeye Medallion (Safe + 10 Bullseyes)

This is the most technical part of the **far far west canyon medallions** run. On the west side, you’ll find a safe with a visible count. You must shoot nearby bullseye targets to reduce the count to zero and unlock the safe.

| Hawkeye Puzzle Component | What to Do |
|---|---|
| **Safe counter** | Track remaining targets |
| **Bullseye boards** | Shoot each one once |
| **Vertical placements** | Check rooftops, rock tops, and zipline-adjacent areas |
| **Final unlock** | Safe opens when all required targets are hit |

Expect at least one target to be awkwardly placed above mine cart lines or elevated structures. Sweep left-to-right, then top-down to avoid missing the final board.
