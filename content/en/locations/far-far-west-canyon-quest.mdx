## Canyon Route Walkthrough: All 4 Medallion Locations

The canyon secret begins near a locked door and a sheriff clue. The note hints at three partners tied to the remaining medals. Instead of decoding vague text, use this direct path.

### 1) Sheriff Medallion (Free pickup)

At the secret door area, inspect the sheriff location and collect the first medallion. This is the easiest step and confirms your run has started correctly.

### 2) Gold Prospector Medallion (Bridge/Cliff Dig Site)

Move toward the lower bridge area and drop down. You will find the prospector zone with several digging spots. Most pits are decoys.

| Landmark | What You See | What To Do |
|---|---|---|
| **Under-bridge zone** | Prospector body/quest trail | **Enter dig area** |
| **Multiple pits** | Several skull-marked holes | **Ignore most skull pits** |
| **Southern dig point** | Distinct non-decoy placement | **Dig for the medallion** |

A shovel interaction is part of this segment, so check the nearby prompt before leaving the cliffside.

### 3) Bobby the Dynamiteer Medallion (Collapsed Mine)

Head to the mine section under broken bridge structures. You will notice blocked gray rock formations inside the shaft.

- Grab dynamite at the mine entrance point.
- Light and place explosives on gray blockages.
- Push deeper after each cleared wall.
- Claim the medallion at the end chamber.

> **Warning:** Do not waste dynamite throws on non-breakable surfaces. Aim only at gray collapse rocks to keep this section quick.

### 4) Hawkeye Medallion (Safe Puzzle with 10 Targets)

This is usually the slowest piece of the far far west canyon quest. Find the safe with a visible counter set to **10**. You must shoot/hit ten hidden bullseye boards in the nearby area to reduce the count and open the safe.

Common target placements include:
- Above and around the safe rock cluster
- Under/near zipline supports
- Top of nearby wooden structures
- Mine cart side structures
- Elevated center position above carts

When the counter reaches zero, the safe opens and you can collect Hawkeye’s medallion.

## Opening the Secret Door and Claiming Rewards

## Combat Strategy After Unlock: Best Uses in 2026

Completing the far far west canyon quest is not just about checking a box. You should immediately fold the rewards into your normal combat loop.

### Elder Pickaxe Usage Pattern

1. **Bait a predictable attack** from elites or bosses.  
2. **Step into close range** as their recovery starts.  
3. **Commit to one heavy sequence** rather than panic swings.  
4. **Disengage and reset spacing** after the burst lands.

Because movement can feel restricted while carrying this style of weapon, use map traversal tools (like carts where available) to reduce downtime between encounters.

### Saloon Boss Phase Advice

The secret pickaxe is especially strong during boss phases that include fixed re-entry paths or repeated approach angles. Time your strike for the moment the target returns to your range.

| Boss Situation | Best Action | Why It Works |
|---|---|---|
| **Boss dashes out/in** | Hold center angle, pre-aim burst | Catches predictable re-entry |
| **Short vulnerability window** | One committed heavy chain | Maximizes damage per opening |
| **Crowded arena** | Clear small threats first | Prevents interrupted swings |

Avoid overextending if you miss the opening. Reset and wait for the next cycle rather than forcing low-value hits.

  Fix: Treat it as a timing weapon, not a spam weapon.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I complete the far far west canyon quest on any difficulty?**

A: Yes. The quest steps and reward path are available on any difficulty. If you are learning locations, run Easy or Normal first for cleaner routing.

**Q: How many medallions are required to open the canyon secret door?**

A: You need four total: sheriff, prospector, dynamiteer, and Hawkeye. Insert all four at the locked door to unlock the chamber.

**Q: Why is the safe not opening after I hit several targets?**

A: The safe puzzle requires all ten bullseyes in the area. One of the elevated or hidden boards is usually missed. Recheck overhead positions near mine carts and wooden structures.
