## How to Read Symbol Order Correctly

The single biggest failure point in **far far west area 41** is order interpretation. Players often enter symbols in discovery order (first region visited to last region visited). That usually doesn’t work.

Use this process instead:

1. Record all four symbols.
2. Record each symbol’s dot indicator.
3. Sort symbols by dot sequence (left-to-right logic).
4. Enter symbols on Area 41 dials in that sorted order.
5. Press confirm once, then wait for feedback.

|---|---|
