### 5) Pool in the open

### 6) Under the water tower
From the pool, keep heading northeast a short distance. The next grave sits beneath a water tower. This pair (5 and 6) is a high-efficiency combo because travel distance is tiny.

### 7) Cactus and rock pocket
Continue to a cluster of cactus and boulders. The grave is tucked between them. Use a short sweep pattern left-to-right if you miss it on first glance.

### 8) Pyramid-style stairs

### 9) Eastern islands wooden structure
Move east toward island terrain with a wooden structure. Check behind or beneath that structure instead of approaching from the front side. That angle is cleaner and safer.

### 10) Southern ravine with rope span
Finish by traveling south to a ravine. Drop into the depression near the rope-covered crossing. The final grave is down there, completing all **Far Far West desert grave locations** in the desert set.
