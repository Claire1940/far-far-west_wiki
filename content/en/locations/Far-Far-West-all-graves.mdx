## Full Grave-by-Grave Breakdown

Use this section as your precise run sheet. If you already know half the map, skim for the two commonly missed spots: the western outhouse and the final northern cave.

### 1) Southern starting grave
Spawn in and push toward the lowest southern boundary. This first soul is straightforward and anchors your entire run.

### 2) Cliffside grave north of the south point
Move north a short distance toward cliff faces. You may need light parkour unless your build has strong vertical mobility. Don’t overcommit jumps with low stamina.

### 3) Beach town rear-building grave
Drop down and follow the train tracks until you reach a small town by the shore. Check behind one of the seaside buildings for the next interaction.

### 4) Rail-adjacent rock grave
Return to the tracks and continue north. A grave is tucked beside rocks very close to the rails. Easy to pass if you sprint without scanning.

### 5) Burned city near the saloon
Shift toward the burned settlement area. Look around the major saloon-style structure for the grave nearby.

### 6) Western edge outhouse grave
Travel significantly west toward the map boundary. The grave/soul point is in an outhouse area. This is one of the most “out of route” locations if you don’t plan ahead.

### 7) Willow area island-edge grave
Go north toward willow trees, crossing past the tracks. One grave sits behind a willow near the map outskirts on a small island-like section.

### 8) Middle map UFO/tunnel grave
Rotate toward the center. Find a broken UFO landmark and a nearby small tunnel; the grave is associated with this pocket.

### 9) Northern sea rocks grave
Push fully north to the coast. Search behind rock clusters near the sea for grave number nine.

### 10) Hidden cave final grave (updated position)
From the northern track endpoint area, drop toward the new cave entrance. Once inside, immediately check the **left side**. That’s the final interaction for the updated **Far Far West all graves** route.

> **Warning:** Players familiar with earlier test versions often miss this because the final grave position changed. Treat the cave as mandatory, even if your memory says otherwise.
