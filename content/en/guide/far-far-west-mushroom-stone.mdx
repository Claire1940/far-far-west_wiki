## Understanding the Woodlands Mushroom Puzzle

The Woodlands map features a unique environmental puzzle that rewards diligent explorers with significant upgrades. Unlike the standard tomb collection quests found in other regions, this area requires you to interact with seven specific mushrooms in a set order before you can access the central hub.

> **💡 Important:** You must light the seven mushrooms in the correct numerical order (1 through 7). The central location, which houses the stone, is the final destination and is not part of the initial numbered sequence.

## Activating the Healing Shroom Tweak

Once you have successfully lit all seven mushrooms, your next goal is the central map room. Reaching this area requires navigating the verticality of the Woodlands, often utilizing available zip lines or climbing ropes to reach the elevated platforms where the **far far west mushroom stone** is situated.

Upon reaching the stone, interacting with it grants you the Healing Shroom tweak. This specialized ability provides a passive or active healing benefit, which can be the difference between success and failure during challenging missions. Because this upgrade is tied to the specific completion of the mushroom sequence, it is highly recommended that new players prioritize this task early in their playthrough to maximize their survivability.
