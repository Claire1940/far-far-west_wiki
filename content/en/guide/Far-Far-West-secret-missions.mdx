## Step-by-Step Walkthrough for Every Map

### 1) Far West: Church Bell Restoration (Bellshot Joker)

Go to the church near the southeast area. You’ll meet the Churchkeeper and find the Great Bell on the tower.  
To restore it:

1. Visit the three smaller bell sites.
2. Interact with the floor trigger at each site.
3. Watch the bell spawn order.
4. Shoot bells in that exact order.
5. Repeat for all three locations.
6. Return to the church and interact with the Great Bell.

**Reward effect:** each Joker has a chance to spawn a bell above enemies; shooting it heals you.

> **Warning:** Don’t rush your shots during the sequence step. A clean memory read is faster than resetting the mini-game multiple times.

---

### 2) Canyon: Four-Medallion Cave Puzzle (Pickpic Joker)

Start at the cave south of center map with four medallion slots. One medallion is on the sheriff’s body nearby; three require mini-events.

- **East bridge zone:** pick up a shovel and dig suspicious mounds (rocky dirt piles) until you find the medallion.
- **North red-rock zone:** use lit dynamite to break glowing rocks until you reach the hidden medallion.
- **West target zone:** shoot all target sets (10) to unlock a safe containing the final medallion.

Return and insert all four medallions at the cave.

**Bonus loot:** this area can also provide strong gold value and a fast-mining pick reward loop in repeated runs.

---

### 3) Desert: Cactus Family Matching (Cactus Day Joker)

This is simple logic plus heavy travel. You must pair 6 cactus children with their 6 parents.

- Pick up a child cactus.
- Use clue text to locate its parent (or reverse: parent clue to child).
- Deliver correctly.
- Repeat until all six pairs are completed.

Because this is a major travel mission, mount mobility and route planning matter more than combat.

---

### 4) Woodlands: Mushroom Circuit Order (Shroom Grave Joker)

Find the stone map in the cave slightly east of center. It shows a number sequence.  
Your job is to activate seven mushrooms in that exact order around the map edge (starting southeast, then rotating).

After touching all seven correctly, return to the stone to complete.

**Reward effect:** mushroom spawns can heal on kill, but with visual side effects that may disrupt aim.

---

### 5) Far Far North: Rebuild the Snowman (Carriable Skin)

In the northern cave, find the incomplete snowman.  
Locate and roll back three parts: base, torso, and head.

Two pieces are close; one is farther away and easier to snag on terrain. Use open lanes and avoid tight tree gaps on return pathing.

Complete assembly for the cosmetic skin reward.

---

### 6) Area 41: Monolith Symbol Sequence (Anti-Gravity Falls Joker)

In Area 41, monolith hints reference four biomes in order:

1. Frontier (Far West)
2. Sand (Desert)
3. Cliffs (Canyon)
4. Trees (Woodlands)

Read/collect the symbol linked to each map, then input in this order at Area 41 and confirm interaction.

**Reward effect:** on enemy death, low chance to spawn anti-gravity orb that launches you upward.
