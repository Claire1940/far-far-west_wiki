## far far west all medallions Route (Best Order)

To minimize travel, collect medallions in a loop that starts near the secret door and finishes at the west-side safe.

### 1) Sheriff Medallion (Quick Pickup)

At the secret-door location in the southern middle section of Canyon, check the sheriff area first. You’ll find one medallion here with minimal effort. This is your anchor point for the whole run, since you’ll return here later anyway.

### 2) Gold Prospector Medallion (Buried Cache)

Move toward the under-bridge route in the southern section. This segment has multiple dig spots, and many are decoys marked with skull imagery. The correct dig point for the medallion is in the southern dig area tied to the prospector path.

A shovel pickup is part of this segment, so make sure you interact with the proper tool spot before digging.

> **Warning:** Don’t assume every dig site is random loot. Several spots are intentional misdirection. Follow the prospector line and commit to the southern correct cache.

### 3) Dynamiteer Medallion (Mine Breach)

Next, push to the mine shaft below a broken bridge landmark. You’ll see blocked passages with gray rock walls. Carry and light dynamite, then chain your way through each blocked section until you reach the medallion chamber.

This is more mechanical than difficult, but wasted movement adds up if you don’t commit to a direct push through the shaft.

### 4) Hawkeye Medallion (Safe Puzzle)

The west-side safe challenge is typically the slowest part of a **far far west all medallions** clear. The safe displays a count (starting at 10), and you must hit nearby bullseye targets to reduce that number to zero and unlock it.

Some targets are obvious; others are elevated or tucked near structures, rock outcrops, and mine-cart lines. Keep a systematic scan pattern:

1. Safe-front and immediate rock line  
2. Under zipline/rail visuals  
3. Rooftops and wooden tower tops  
4. Mine-cart endpoint structures  
5. High centerline above cart lanes

When all targets are hit, open the safe and take the Hawkeye medallion.
