## Optimized Build for Rapid Leveling

To truly master the tutorial farm and maximize your **Far Far West xp tickets** efficiency, you need a build focused on two things: raw damage for the boss and extreme mobility for the traversal.

### Weapon Choice
The **Shotgun** is the undisputed king for this farming method. Its high burst damage allows you to delete the tutorial boss, the Lich, almost instantly. To optimize this, ensure you have the full damage upgrade path unlocked. Since you aren't worried about long-term survival in the tutorial, you can ignore defensive mods in favor of pure lethality.

### Essential Spells
Spells are the secret sauce to cutting your run time down.
*   **Boing:** This spell provides the verticality needed to skip platforming sections and navigate the environment faster than running.
*   **Portal:** This is perhaps the most important tool for the Lich fight. By placing one portal at the boss summon location and another at the extraction point, you can kill the boss and teleport instantly to the exit, shaving 15-20 seconds off your total time.

### Hero Stat Allocation
When spending your hero points for a farming build, prioritize movement over everything else.

| Stat | Priority | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Movement Speed** | **High** | Decreases time between objectives. |
| **Jump Height** | **High** | Allows for shortcuts over obstacles. |
| **Damage** | **Medium** | Necessary only for the final boss kill. |
| **Health/Defense** | **Low** | Not needed for the "Easy" tutorial difficulty. |

## Advanced Speedrunning Perks

To reach sub-3-minute runs, you need to equip perks that enhance your dash and air control. The goal is to stay airborne and moving forward as much as possible.

1.  **Moon Gravity (Stack x2):** Lowering your gravity allows your dashes to carry you much further through the air. Instead of a short burst that ends when you hit the ground, a low-gravity dash lets you glide across large sections of the map.
2.  **Speedy Gonzales:** A straightforward buff to your movement speed that stacks with other bonuses.
3.  **Disgrace:** While this perk might have social consequences in the game world (like Grand Roach refusing to help you), the movement speed bonus it provides is too good to pass up for a dedicated XP farmer.

> **⚠️ Warning:** While you can "corrupt" the Lich boss using certain abilities, this does not count as a kill. You must actually deplete the Lich's health bar to trigger the mission completion and receive your XP.
