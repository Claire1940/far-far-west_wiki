## Route Planning: How to Convert Souls Into Faster Wins

Good economy is not only about what you buy—it’s also where and when you fight. New region flow, hidden objectives, and challenge layers reward intentional routing.

### Practical Route Framework

1. **Open with quick-clear zones** to secure early souls and momentum.  
2. **Complete one core objective path** before detouring for optional value.  
3. **Use side objectives only if your build can clear without stalling.**  
4. **Before boss chain, spend to cover one weakness** (mobility, sustain, or burst).  
5. **After completion, continue run only if your loadout scales.**

This is especially important now that you can jump into another match faster after mission completion. Chaining runs magnifies the value of efficient Far Far West souls use.

### Modifier Decision Rule (Hard Mode)
