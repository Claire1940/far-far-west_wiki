## Far Far West jokers: Complete Map-by-Map Unlock Guide

This section is your core checklist for unlocking all **Far Far West jokers** efficiently.

### 1) Far West Map — Bellshot Joker

Head to the church near the southeast side. Interact with the great bell at the top to begin the objective, then visit three bell sites around the map.

At each site:
1. Interact with the floor trigger.
2. Watch the bell spawn order.
3. Shoot bells in that exact sequence.
4. Repeat if you fail.

After all three are solved, return to the church and interact with the great bell to claim **Bellshot**.

**Effect summary:** each joker has a 5% chance to spawn a bell above enemies; shooting it heals you for 20 HP.

### 2) Canyon Map — Pickpic Joker

Find the cave with four medallion slots south of center. One medallion is on the sheriff corpse nearby. The other three require mini-challenges:

- **East side:** dig suspect mounds near bridge shovels until medallion appears.
- **North side:** blow up red glowing rocks with lit dynamite.
- **West side:** shoot all target sets (10) to open the safe.

Insert all four medallions at the cave to finish and unlock **Pickpic**.

**Effect summary:** each joker has a 5% chance to apply a pickaxe-like damage-over-time proc on hit (extra DPS feel, especially with rapid-fire weapons).

### 3) Desert Map — Cactus Day Joker

You must reunite six cactus children with six cactus parents. This is straightforward but time-consuming. NPC-like hints from each cactus can guide you, but route knowledge is much faster.

Return each small cactus to its correct large cactus to complete the mission and earn **Cactus Day**.

**Effect summary:** each joker has a 3% on-kill chance to spawn cactus mines that explode and can reset, giving useful area denial.

### 4) Woodlands Map — Shroom Grave Joker

Find the central/eastern cave stone that shows a number sequence. Follow map points in that exact order:
- Start southeast.
- Move clockwise around edge points.
- Touch all required mushrooms in sequence.
- Return to stone to claim reward.

You’ll unlock **Shroom Grave**.

**Effect summary:** 10% on-kill chance to spawn healing shrooms for 10 HP, with a vision-distortion side effect.

### 5) Far Far North Map — Skin Unlock

Inside the north cave, interact with the unfinished snowman. Retrieve body sections from three marked positions and roll them back manually. The lower/body piece is the longest push and easiest to get stuck on terrain.

Completing this grants a **carriable skin**, not a joker.

### 6) Area 41 — Anti-Gravity Falls Joker

In Area 41, interact with monoliths and enter symbols in map-order logic:
- Frontier (Far West)
- Sand (Desert)
- Cliffs (Canyon)
- Trees (Woodlands)

After correct input, activate the button to obtain **Anti-Gravity Falls**.

**Effect summary:** 2% on-kill chance to spawn anti-gravity spheres that lift you upward; niche utility, limited direct combat value.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the best Far Far West jokers to unlock first?**

A: Start with Bellshot, then Pickpic. Bellshot improves sustain quickly, and Pickpic adds passive damage that feels strong on automatic weapons.

**Q: Is the Far Far North secret mission required for all Far Far West jokers?**

A: It’s part of the secret mission set, but it gives a carriable skin instead of a weapon joker. You can delay it if your priority is combat strength.

**Q: Which Far Far West jokers are best for crowd clearing?**

A: Cactus Day is the most AoE-friendly thanks to explosive mine procs on kills. Pair it with a fast-kill weapon setup for better uptime.

**Q: Why does Shroom Grave feel weaker than expected?**

A: It has a good trigger rate, but each shroom heal is small and the visual distortion can interfere with tracking. It’s useful, just more situational than Bellshot.
