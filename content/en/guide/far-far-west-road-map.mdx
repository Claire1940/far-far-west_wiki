## Unlocking Gear: The Fragment System

A common mistake new players make is assuming that weapon unlocks are automatic. On the **far far west road map**, you must be proactive. Weapon fragments are dropped by bosses, but they are not universal. You must manually set which weapon or utility you are currently targeting in your inventory menu.

If you defeat a boss while your fragment target is set to a weapon you already own, you will begin collecting fragments for skins instead. While skins are a great way to customize your hunter, missing out on a functional weapon upgrade because you forgot to swap your target can stall your progress for several runs.

1.  **Open the Equipment Menu** – Check your current fragment target.
2.  **Select an Unlocked Tier** – Choose the weapon you wish to build toward.
3.  **Defeat Area Bosses** – Collect the dropped fragments to fill the progress bar.
4.  **Repeat for Utility Items** – Don't forget that fragments also apply to non-weapon gear.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Once a boss is defeated, the game enters an "Infinite Wave" state. Do not engage the boss until you have completed every side objective on the map, as the difficulty spike makes exploration nearly impossible afterward.

## Advanced Combat Tactics

As you follow the **far far west road map**, you will notice that enemy behavior changes based on your elemental usage. Enemies afflicted by Acid are easier to hit with high-skill-cap spells like Fire Beam because their movement is predictable. Conversely, using Electric attributes is best for "trash" mobs that swarm in high numbers, allowing the lightning to do the work for you.

### XP Optimization

To maximize your spell XP, you should aim for a "rotation" of elements. Even if your build is primarily Pyro-focused, dipping into Voodoo or Cactus once per run ensures you are gaining the maximum possible XP from the one-time use bonuses. This strategy allows you to reach the "Mastery" levels of your primary line much faster than if you ignored the other elements entirely.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I find the best route on the far far west road map?**

A: The best route involves prioritizing side objectives before the main boss. Side objectives often provide the buffs and XP needed to survive the infinite waves that trigger post-boss. Ensure you have your fragment target set correctly before the fight begins.

**Q: Which spell combo is the strongest in the 2026 meta?**

A: Currently, the Flaming Tornado (Fire Beam + Thrower) remains a top-tier choice for raw damage. However, Acid-based combos are gaining popularity for their ability to slow down bosses during the late-game stages of a run.

**Q: Can I change my secondary weapon element during a fight?**

A: Yes, you can switch between Pyro, Acid, and Electric attributes at any time. This is a key mechanic for setting up combos or reacting to enemies with specific elemental weaknesses.

**Q: What happens if I forget to set my fragment target?**

A: If you don't manually select a weapon, the game will default to the last selected item or begin collecting fragments for weapon skins. Always double-check your target in the equipment menu before starting a boss encounter to avoid wasting valuable drops.
