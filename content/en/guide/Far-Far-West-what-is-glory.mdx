## Common mistakes that slow Glory progression

Even strong players can stall if they make these errors repeatedly:

- **Ignoring mission mechanics:** Kills look good, but objectives win runs.
- **Stacking too many modifiers too soon:** High risk with no adaptation plan.
- **No role definition in co-op:** Duplicate roles cause objective failure.
- **Using one loadout everywhere:** Different objectives require different tools.
- **Skipping learning runs:** Practice sessions are not wasted time.

A clean approach is to define one “comfort build,” one “modifier build,” and one “boss build,” then rotate based on mission type.
