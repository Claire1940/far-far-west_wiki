## Far Far West mushroom quest step-by-step route

The core mechanic is simple: follow the numbered sequence from **1 to 7**, activating each mushroom node, then return to the initial stone location.

| Step | Action | What to Watch For |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Find the central stone with numbered clues | Interacting early does not finalize quest |
| **2** | Mount up and ride to marker **1** | Confirm visual activation/glow |
| **3** | Continue through **2, 3, 4, 5, 6** | Keep sequence order consistent |

### Route efficiency notes

Follow these steps for a cleaner **Far Far West mushroom quest** run:

1. Start with full ammo and at least one emergency heal.
2. Use horse sprint on long open stretches; dismount only near activation points.
3. Ignore unnecessary fights unless enemies body-block narrow paths.
4. After node 7, go directly back to the stone—don’t detour for loot.

> **Warning:** If your order gets mixed up, progression can feel inconsistent. Reconfirm the numbered sequence and re-run from the earliest uncertain node.
