## Northern Area 41 Graves Locations

The northern sector of Area 41 is dominated by steep cliffs and murky, radioactive waters. This area contains three distinct graves, one of which is hidden behind a clever environmental puzzle.

### 1. The Hidden Cliffside House
Head to the northernmost point of the map. Look out over the murky waters from the cliff edge. You will notice a narrow path winding along the side of the cliff. Follow this path until you reach a series of wooden boards. This is a jump puzzle that requires careful timing. Once you navigate the boards, you will find a hidden house tucked away in the rock. The first grave is located inside this structure.

### 2. The Central Island
In the middle of the northern basin, there is a small island surrounded by industrial debris. It appears to be a site of significant excavation or construction. You will find the second grave sitting right in the center of this island, amidst the clutter of pipes and machinery.

### 3. The Piano Cliff
Make your way to the high cliffs overlooking the central valley. You are looking for a specific plateau that features a lone piano and several rocking chairs—a surreal sight in the wasteland. The third grave is situated directly behind the piano.

## Industrial and Canyon Grave Sites

The mid-section of the map transitions into deep canyons and abandoned industrial infrastructure. These **area 41 graves** are often tucked away in places where players rarely venture.

| Grave Location | Key Landmark | Difficulty |

| **Acid Pool Canyon** | End of the tire-filled acid stream | Medium |
| **Antenna Bridge** | Under the wooden bridge near the tower | Low |

### 4. The Tire Canyon Acid Pool
This grave is easily missed because it requires you to drop down into a canyon most players simply jump over. Look for a small pool of acid filled with old tires. Follow the stream of acid to its very end, where the canyon walls close in. The grave is located at the terminus of this path.

### 5. The Antenna Tower Undercroft
Locate the massive antenna tower on your map. Instead of climbing up immediately, look beneath the wooden bridge that leads toward the base of the tower. The grave is tucked away in the shadows under the bridge's support beams.

### 6. The Zipline Cabin
Climb the cliff where the antenna tower stands. Use the zipline to cross the gap to a nearby house. Once inside the house, you will see a stack of crates. You must climb over or move these crates to reveal the grave hidden in the back corner of the room.

## Reaching the High Altitude Graves

The seventh grave is arguably the most difficult to reach in the entire region. It requires a multi-stage platforming sequence that will test your patience and your mastery of the game's movement mechanics.

From the cabin where you found the sixth grave, cross the bridge and jump over to the adjacent industrial building. You will find another zipline; take it upward. From here, you must manually climb the giant tower. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not interact with the launch pad at the base of the final tower section; it is designed to shoot you away from the objective rather than toward it.

Climb all the way to the "tippy top" of the tower. You will find several small outhouses. The grave is located inside one of these outhouses, offering a strange final resting place with a view of the entire map.

## Western Shipwrecks and Submerged Sites

The western edge of Area 41 is where the land gives way to a vast, acidic sea. Here, you will find the final three **area 41 graves** among the ruins of old vessels.

### 8. The Deserted Hull
On the western shore, look for the rusted wreck of a large ship. The hull is cracked open, allowing you to walk inside. The eighth grave is located deep within the belly of the ship, resting on the sandy floor of the interior.

### 9. The Submerged House
Further along the coast, you will see a house that has partially sunk into the acid water. You will need to navigate carefully to reach the underside of this structure. The ninth grave is located right beneath the floorboards of the submerged house.

### 10. The Mast Peak
The final grave is visible from the submerged house. It is located at the very top of the mast of a nearby ship wreck. To reach it quickly, using the Thunder Fist is highly recommended. Scale the rigging and the mast to reach the highest point of the ship to claim your final reward.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need special equipment to find all the area 41 graves?**

A: While you can reach many on foot, having the Thunder Fist and the Roach makes the process significantly faster. The high-altitude grave on the tower and the mast grave are much easier to reach with upgraded mobility skills.

**Q: Is there a reward for finding all the graves in Area 41?**

A: Yes, collecting all the graves typically contributes to regional completion percentages, unlocks specific lore entries, and may grant achievements depending on your platform. In the 2026 update, some graves also provide small amounts of rare crafting materials.

**Q: Can I find the graves in any order?**

A: Absolutely. While this guide follows a logical path from North to West, you can collect the **area 41 graves** in whatever order suits your current exploration path. However, the tower grave (Grave 7) is best done after Grave 6 due to their proximity.

**Q: What should I do if I fall into the acid while looking for the submerged graves?**

A: If you have Acid Resistance gear, you should have enough time to jump back to safety. If not, use the Thunder Fist to dash toward the nearest solid ground or wreck immediately. Always keep your health topped off when exploring the western coast.
