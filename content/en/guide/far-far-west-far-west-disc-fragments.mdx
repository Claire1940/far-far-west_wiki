## Detailed Fragment Locations and Navigation

### The Northwest Broken Ship
The first of the **far far west far west disc fragments** is situated in the rugged Northwest sector of the map. To find this, players should head toward the large underpass that serves as a primary navigation point for the shovel digging quest. Once you reach the underpass, continue heading further North. 

The landscape here is characterized by steep cliffs and debris. Look for the silhouette of a broken ship—an unusual sight in the middle of the desert. The fragment is located within the wreckage. This piece was famously known in early builds as the "Mummy" fragment, so do not be confused if your quest log or older community maps refer to it by that name.

### Willy Billy’s House
The second piece is perhaps the easiest to locate because it is tied to one of the game’s most recognizable characters: Willy Billy. Willy Billy is clearly marked on the world map, making him an excellent landmark for navigation. 

To find this fragment:
1. Locate Willy Billy’s icon on your map in the western-central region.
2. Approach his small house/shack.
3. Check directly behind the NPC or within the immediate interior of the house.

It appears Willy Billy was "safekeeping" several pieces of the theme, and this fragment sits right in his living quarters. Players often find this to be the quickest pickup during a standard playthrough.

### The Eastern Town Cart
The final piece of the puzzle requires a trip to the easternmost part of the map. There is a small town located here that serves as a hub for various trading missions. Unlike the other pieces that are found in wreckage or residences, this one is located in a more public space.

Navigate to the town and look for the various transport carts lined up near the entrance or the main thoroughfare. The final fragment is tucked away inside one of these carts. It seems the piece was being prepared for transport or "pawned off" before you arrived. Once you interact with the cart, the collection will be complete.

## Exploration Strategy and Tips

While the locations are fixed, the journey to reach them can be perilous depending on your current level and equipment. In 2026, the meta for exploration involves high-mobility builds that allow you to traverse the desert quickly.

*   **Mount Usage:** Always ensure your mount is fully fed and rested before heading to the Northwest or East, as these are long treks from the central hub.
*   **Map Markers:** Even though Willy Billy has a permanent icon, the Broken Ship does not. Use a custom waypoint once you find it to help other players in your party.
*   **Inventory Check:** Ensure you have at least three open slots in your "Key Items" or "Collectibles" tab before heading out, though the fragments generally do not take up standard inventory space.

> **Warning:** If you collect a fragment and it doesn't appear in your collection, check your UI settings. The recent name change from "Mummy Disc" to "Far Far West Theme" has caused some visual bugs where the item appears under a different category than expected.
