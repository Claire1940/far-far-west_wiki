### 1) West section: Safe + target chain (Hawkeye / Focus medallion)

Find the small rock formation with a safe that has a target attached. Shoot the first target to activate a countdown, then locate the remaining targets in the nearby western area (cliffs, archway vicinity, and elevated points including a tower/top ridge).

**Quick method:**
1. Activate target on safe.
2. Sweep nearby arch and cliff line first.
3. Check elevated tower/peak angles with sidearm.
4. Finish hidden targets around nearby rocks.
5. Return to safe and claim medallion.

This part is where most runs slow down. A medium-range sidearm saves time and frustration.

### 2) North section: Dynamite crack puzzle (Bobby / Luck medallion)

In the northern corner area, locate the cracked rock and nearby dynamite crates. Light and throw dynamite into the crack to break it open. Usually, multiple throws are needed unless your placement is excellent.

**Placement tip:** Aim toward the center depth of the crack rather than the edge to get better structural break progress per throw.

### 3) East section: The Far Far West shovel quest dig phase (Sweaty / Greed medallion)

This is the signature part players mean when they say **Far Far West shovel quest**. Pick up any nearby shovel and dig marked rocky soil patches in the eastern zone until the medallion appears.

| Digging Phase Element | What to Look For | Efficiency Advice |
|---|---|---|
| **Shovel pickup** | Multiple shovel props in the area | Grab the closest and start immediately. |
| **Dig spots** | Small rocky/patchy ground markers | Move in a tight circle pattern, not random zig-zag. |
| **Medallion spawn** | Appears after correct patch is dug | Some players report recurring spawn spots; check common patch first. |

> **Tip:** During the Far Far West shovel quest dig step, clear nearby enemies before digging. Interruptions cost more time than a short combat reset.

### 4) South cave area: Sheriff medallion (Bravery)

Near the cave/door zone, the final medallion is usually straightforward to collect from the sheriff body location. This is often the fastest piece of the chain, but it can be in a danger-heavy lane depending on your objective path.

## Inserting medallions and unlocking the cave reward

## Route optimization, mistakes, and repeat farming strategy

The Far Far West shovel quest is strongest when treated as a routine loop rather than a one-time secret.

### Efficient loop pattern for repeat runs

1. Spawn and identify nearest compass section.
2. Complete closest medallion first (don’t force west opener unless nearby).
3. Keep objectives moving while rotating puzzle steps.
4. Open cave before final boss if safe, then mine fast.
5. Finish mission cleanly.

### Common mistakes that slow players down

- Entering with only short-range tools and wasting time on elevated targets.
- Digging while under heavy aggro instead of stabilizing first.
- Overfarming the cave and losing objective tempo.
- Forgetting that completion matters for practical rewards.

> **Warning:** If your lobby state feels unstable, play the quest safely and avoid greed routing. One lost run can erase the time saved by aggressive shortcuts.
